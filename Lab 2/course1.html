Academic English 2

This course aims to develop ability in writing and oral presentation for academic purposes. We learn about the skills required in writing a variety of texts in our own relevant fields of study and also learn how language functions in the academic genres covered.

Learning Outcome

Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to demonstrate the following skills:

  1. Use APA referencing style to cite sources (C3)
  2. Produce written academic texts in the genres covered by using various paraphrasing techniques (A2)
  3. Explain academic topics orally using appropriate language and presentation skills (A3)

Activities

Activity Total Marks(%)
Assessment 1a: Oral Presentation (Info Report) 15
Assessment 1b: Information Report (Written) 20
Assessment 2a: Oral Presentation (Explanation Report) 15
Assessment 2b: Explanation Report (Written) 20
Final Examamination 30
