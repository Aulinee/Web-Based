Introduction to Green Technology

This course serves as an introduction to why research in sustainable and alternative energies is so important today. We will how alternative resources such as solar, wind, nuclear, biomass and hydropower are used to produce energy.

Learning Outcome

By the end of this course, students should be able to:

  1. Describe the history, global, environmental and economical impacts of green technology
  2. Demonstrate the impact of technological solutions in societal and environmental contexts and demonstrate knowledge of sustainable development
  3. Prepare in independent and life-long learning in the broadest context of technological change
  4. Explain the various method of energy generation & their recent advancement for conventional and alternative energy sources and identify the various energy saving methods
